# 277dao's Seasons 1-3

[277dao's blog](https://paragraph.com/@277dao)

In the early part of 2022, the Dark Forest universe witnessed a series of captivating rounds hosted by the Chinese DAO, 277dao. These three consecutive seasons, while maintaining the traditional scoring mechanisms, brought a fresh wave of excitement and competition to the community.

What set these rounds apart was the significant prize pool offered in xDAI, a factor that attracted a wide array of players, eager to claim their share of the rewards. But 277dao's initiative wasn't just about the grandeur of prizes; it offered a nod to the classic Dark Forest Prize Planets, awarding the top contenders their distinct interpretation which can be viewed in their [collection](https://opensea.io/collection/277dao-df). This approach mirrored the allure of the renowned [Valhalla](https://opensea.io/collection/darkforest-valhalla) Prize Planets, creating a link between traditional elements and new ventures.

Enhancing the round's appeal was the organizer's novel approach to in-game item exchanging for xDAI prizes. Previously, player-to-player exchanges of xDAI were common, but 277dao introduced a new dynamic by distributing rewards to players for these in-game items. This initiative opened a new avenue for rewards, extending beyond the traditional player-driven market dynamics. It allowed participants from all levels, particularly those with smaller empires, to actively engage with the prize pool. This shift was more than just an economic innovation; it was a strategic inclusion by the organizers to ensure a more equitable distribution of rewards, recognizing and valuing the efforts of a diverse range of players, not solely those at the top of the leaderboard.

Another pioneering effort by 277dao was their sponsorship of gas for players. Recognizing the challenges faced by new players, especially those transitioning to a different blockchain, 277dao provided xDAI for gas to whitelisted accounts. This gesture not only eased the financial burden for newcomers but also showcased a commitment to growing the Dark Forest community by making the game more accessible.

Through these three seasons, 277dao demonstrated a blend of generosity and innovation. Whilst the core gameplay remained familiar, their approach to prizes and player support marked a significant contribution to the evolving narrative of Dark Forest, making these rounds a memorable chapter in the game's history.

# DFDAO's Galactic League/Grand Prix

[dfdao's blog](https://medium.com/dfdao)

September 2022 saw DFDAO introducing a groundbreaking concept to the Dark Forest universe with their Galactic League/Grand Prix - a single-player speedrun competition that deviated significantly from the traditional MMO-RPG style gameplay. This event brought an electrifying twist, transforming the familiar multiplayer strategy setting into a fast-paced, individual race against time and space.

<figure>
  <img
    src="/images/blog/dark-forest-revision/part-2/5.avif"

</figure>

In this unique format, each participant was thrust into an identical mini universe, freshly redeployed at the start of every race. The challenge lay in navigating this universe as quickly and efficiently as possible, with competitors vying to find optimal pathways. The races were brief yet intense, lasting anywhere from 2 to 20 minutes, depending on the map, and the speed and strategy of the runners.

Adding to the competitive fervor was an upheaval to the scoring mechanism, which was tested during the season. This update encouraged players to complete their runs using fewer moves combined with fast times, adding a layer of tactical depth to the races. The emphasis on efficiency and precision made the Grand Prix not just a test of speed, but of strategic planning and quick decision-making.

DFDAO's Grand Prix boldly stood out as a fresh and innovative addition to the Dark Forest lineup. It wasn't just a race; it was a high-speed chase through the cosmos, blending the thrill of speedrunning with the strategic complexity of Dark Forest. This round was a testament to the game's versatility and the community's appetite for diverse and dynamic gameplay experiences.
